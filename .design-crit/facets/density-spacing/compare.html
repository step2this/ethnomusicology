C Open in new tab ↗
Session
48px rows ~16 tracks visible 4px base unit
WHY THIS OPTION
Most spacious. Meets Material 3 touch targets. Larger art, wider chips. Comfortable for extended sessions and tablet use.
WATCH OUT FOR
16 visible tracks means more scrolling. Feels more like a music app than pro DJ software. Extra whitespace dilutes the dense-tool aesthetic.
MY TAKE
B (Studio) is the clear winner

This is not a close call. Option B (Studio) is the density you've been reviewing and approving throughout the entire crit — 40px rows, 32x32 art, 4px base unit. It's dense enough to feel like professional DJ software while giving the Camelot chips and energy bars room to do their job.

A is too tight — it sacrifices the very data viz that makes this product distinctive. C is too loose — it loses the pro-tool density you've consistently valued.

The 4px base unit with a 7-token scale (4/8/12/16/24/32/48) gives clean, predictable spacing that aligns with everything already locked.
