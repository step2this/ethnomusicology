Mixer Board
14 components, DJ-hardware inspired. Import as a right slide-over panel (400px) with scrim. Track rows: click to select with left accent border + expandable detail strip below for secondary metadata (label, year, album, waveform preview). Camelot as rounded square, energy as LED dots. Multi-column sort with priority numbers on headers.
